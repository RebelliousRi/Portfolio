Privacy Policy for Campus Eats
Effective Date: 17/12/25
Last Updated: 17/12/24
At Campus Eats, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect the personal information of our customers, vendors, and users. By accessing or using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Collect
We collect the following types of information based on your interactions with our platform:
1.1 For Customers
Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, delivery address.
Order Details: Food preferences, special instructions, and order history.
Payment Information: Credit card details and payment method (secured by third-party payment processors).
1.2 For Vendors
Business Information: Vendor name, contact details, menu items, pricing, and license documentation.
Account Information: Login credentials and banking details for payment processing.
Performance Data: Customer reviews, order metrics, and transaction history.
1.3 For General Users
Browsing Data: IP addresses, device information, cookies, and usage data for analytics and performance tracking.
Feedback: Reviews, ratings, and customer support queries.

2. How We Use Your Information
2.1 For Customers
To process and fulfill orders.
To communicate order updates, confirmations, and customer service responses.
To provide personalized food recommendations based on order history.
2.2 For Vendors
To manage vendor accounts and process payments.
To showcase menu items and vendor profiles.
To improve customer satisfaction by sharing anonymized feedback.
2.3 For General Users
To enhance user experience through website optimization.
To improve service offerings based on usage analytics.
To notify users of updates, promotions, or changes in terms.

3. Sharing of Information
We may share your information in the following circumstances:
With Vendors: Customer information is shared with vendors to fulfill orders.
With Third-Party Providers: Payment processors, delivery partners, or analytics providers who assist in service delivery.
As Required by Law: When necessary to comply with legal obligations or protect rights and safety.
Campus Eats does not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Security
We implement robust measures to safeguard your information, including encryption for sensitive data, secure servers, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights
5.1 Access and Updates
You can access and update your personal information through your account settings.
5.2 Deletion Requests
Customers and vendors may request the deletion of their accounts and associated data by contacting our support team.
5.3 Opt-Out
Users may opt out of marketing communications by adjusting their preferences in account settings or following the opt-out link in emails.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies
We use cookies to enhance user experience, analyze site performance, and provide personalized content. Users can control cookie preferences through browser settings.

7. Data Retention
We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations. After this period, data is securely deleted or anonymized.
