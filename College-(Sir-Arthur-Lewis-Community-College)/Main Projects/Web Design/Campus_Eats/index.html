About Us

Welcome to Campus Eats, your one-stop shop for seamless food ordering on campus. We cater to students, lecturers, staff, and visitors, by simplifying the process with updated menus and convenient ordering options. Say goodbye to long lines and wasted time! Our dedicated team shares a vision to modernize food ordering on campus and is committed to enhancing campus life and providing a user-friendly experience. Our goal is to become the ultimate campus dining solution, with plans for loyalty programs, and eco-friendly initiatives.

Our Vendors

Abi's Doubles

Le Forte

The Caf

Abi's Doubles serves authentic Trinidadian street food, specializing in doubles, rotis, and other Indo-Caribbean favorites. This family-run stand is known for its bold spices, fresh ingredients, and warm, friendly service, offering a true taste of Trinidad crafted with pride and care.

Located on the Morne Fortune campus of Sir Arthur Lewis Community College, Le Forte is a student-run restaurant blending St. Lucian heritage with global cuisine. Using fresh, local ingredients, it showcases creative dishes prepared by aspiring chefs in a welcoming atmosphere with stunning views of Castries.

The Caf is a family-operated fast-food truck offering comfort foods like burgers, fries, and local specials. Nestled outside the owners' home, it provides a cozy environment with exceptional service, making it a popular choice for quick, satisfying meals on campus.
